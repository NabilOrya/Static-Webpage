    • Introduction

    The Ghulam Ishaq Khan Institute of Engineering Sciences and Technology, or GIK Institute, is a prominent engineering university in Pakistan. GIK, which is situated in Topi, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, provides undergraduate and graduate courses in business, science, and engineering. The institute was founded in 1988 and places a strong emphasis on innovation, high-quality research, and education. The GIK Institute is renowned for its cutting-edge facilities, research achievements, and strict academic standards. The institution promotes an atmosphere that is favorable to education, growth, and technological advancement. Welcome to the vibrant Sports Complex, on of the most important aspect of student life at Ghulam Ishaq Khan Institute, where students engage in a variety of sports and physical activities. The Sports Complex is not just a place for games; it's a hub for fostering a healthy and active lifestyle among students.

    About Sports Complex

    There is a state-of-the-art sports complex at the Ghulam Ishaq Khan Institute of Engineering Sciences and Technology (GIKI). The Sports Complex at GIKI offers state-of-the-art facilities to cater to the diverse interests of students. From indoor arenas to outdoor fields, we provide an environment that encourages physical well-being and sportsmanship.

    Facilities include:

    • The multifunctional sports hall, gymnasium, squash courts, tennis courts, and swimming pool are all part of the GIKI sports complex.

    Variety of Sports Activities:

    • It provides a selection of sports like badminton, table tennis, football, cricket, basketball, and athletics.

    Intramural and Intercollegiate Competitions:

    • GIKI promotes student participation in intramural and intercollegiate competitions to help students develop a sense of teamwork and competition.

    Fitness and Recreation:

    • The facility provides a central location for students to stay in shape, have fun, and relax from the demands of their studies.

    Expert Coaching:

    • Students interested in improving their talents in a variety of sports can receive expert coaching and advice from GIKI's sports complex.
